CPR Steps

  • 1

    Check Responsiveness

    Tap and shout to check if the person is responsive

  • 2

    Call Emergency Services

    Call 108 or ask someone to call while you begin CPR

  • 3

    Begin Chest Compressions

    Place hands on center of chest, push hard and fast at 100-120 compressions per minute

  • 4

    Give Rescue Breaths

    After 30 compressions, give 2 rescue breaths

  • 5

    Continue Until Help Arrives

    Repeat cycles of 30 compressions and 2 breaths until emergency services arrive
